### [1] [Asymmetric Generative Recommendation via Multi-Expert Projection and Multi-Faceted Hierarchical Quantization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14512)
*Bin Huang,Xin Wang,Junwei Pan,Yongqi Zhou,Yifeng Zhou,Zhixiang Feng,Shudong Huang,Haijie Gu,Wenwu Zhu*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文针对现有对称式生成推荐（GenRec）存在的双阶段信息瓶颈问题，提出了非对称框架AsymRec，通过多专家语义投影和多面层次量化分别优化输入输出，实验结果相比现有最优生成推荐模型平均提升15.8%。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有生成推荐模型将项目表示为离散语义ID，对称用作输入和预测目标，存在两个关键信息瓶颈：输入瓶颈中，有损量化会降低细粒度语义质量，流行度偏差会让学习到的表示偏向高频项目；输出瓶颈中，不精确的离散目标会限制监督质量。因此需要解决该问题来提升生成推荐性能。

Method: 提出非对称连续-离散框架AsymRec，解耦输入和输出表示：1. 多专家语义投影（MSP），通过专家专用投影将连续嵌入映射到Transformer隐藏空间，保留语义丰富度并提升对低频项目的泛化能力；2. 多面层次量化（MHQ），通过带语义正则化的多视图多层量化构建大容量结构化离散目标，防止维度崩塌同时保留细粒度区分度。

Result: 大量实验证明，AsymRec相比现有最优生成推荐模型性能 consistently 更优，平均提升了15.8%，代码将会开源。

Conclusion: 非对称输入输出设计结合多专家投影和多面层次量化，可以有效解决对称生成推荐中的双阶段信息瓶颈问题，显著提升生成推荐性能。

Abstract: Generative Recommendation (GenRec) models reformulate recommendation as a sequence generation task, representing items as discrete Semantic IDs used symmetrically as both inputs and prediction targets. We identify a critical dual-stage information bottleneck in this design: (1) the Input Bottleneck, where lossy quantization degrades fine-grained semantics, while popularity bias skews the learned representations toward frequent items, and (2) the Output Bottleneck, where imprecise discrete targets limit supervision quality. To address these issues, we propose AsymRec, an asymmetric continuous-discrete framework that decouples input and output representations. Specifically, Multi-expert Semantic Projection (MSP) maps continuous embeddings into the Transformer's hidden space via expert-specialized projections, preserving semantic richness and improving generalization to infrequent items. Multi-faceted Hierarchical Quantization (MHQ) constructs high-capacity, structured discrete targets through multi-view and multi-level quantization with semantic regularization, preventing dimensional collapse while retaining fine-grained distinctions. Extensive experiments demonstrate that AsymRec consistently outperforms state-of-the-art generative recommenders by an average of 15.8 %. The code will be released.

### [2] [Discrimination Is Generation: Unifying Ranking and Retrieval from a Tokenizer Perspective](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14853)
*Shuli Wang,Junwei Yin,Changhao Li,Senjie Kou,Chi Wang,Yinqiu Huang,Yinhua Zhu,Haitao Wang,Xingxing Wang*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文针对生成式推荐中语义ID（SIDs）分词器与个性化信号 decouple的问题，提出统一框架DIG，将分词器嵌入判别排序模型进行端到端训练，一次训练同时得到排序和检索模型，在公开数据集和工业数据集上实验验证了该方法对排序、检索及统一任务性能的提升。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 生成式推荐中，语义ID（SIDs）决定了个性化上限，但现有分词器基于检索目标独立训练，导致个性化信号与SID构建过程完全解耦，这一基础差距使得生成式检索性能持续落后于判别式排序，因此需要解决该问题。

Method: 文章首先提出核心观点：排序是物品空间的argmax问题，检索是词元空间的argmax问题，二者本质是同一问题在不同粒度上的求解。进而提出DIG框架，围绕特征分配分类法组织：将物品固有静态特征编码到SIDs，用户-物品交叉特征在训练中隐式推动码本边界靠近推荐决策边界，使用MLP_u2t蒸馏模块在推理时在词元层级近似用户-物品交叉特征，将分词器嵌入判别排序模型做端到端训练，一次训练同时得到排序模型和检索模型。

Result: 在三个公开基准数据集和两个工业数据集上的实验结果表明，DIG同时提升了排序、检索以及统一检索-排序的性能。

Conclusion: 排序和检索本质是同一问题在不同粒度上的求解，所提出的DIG统一框架通过端到端训练将分词器嵌入判别排序模型，能够同时提升排序、检索和统一任务的效果，证明了该统一思路的有效性。

Abstract: Semantic IDs (SIDs) define the generation space of generative recommendation and directly determine its personalization ceiling. However, existing tokenizers are trained independently with retrieval objectives, leaving personalization signals fully decoupled from the SID construction process -- a fundamental gap that causes generative retrieval to persistently lag behind discriminative ranking. In this paper, we rethink the essence of SIDs: \emph{ranking seeks argmax in item space while retrieval seeks argmax in token space; both are the same problem solved at different granularities.} Based on this insight, we propose \DIG (\textbf{D}iscrimination \textbf{I}s \textbf{G}eneration), which embeds the tokenizer inside a discriminative ranking model for end-to-end training -- the ranker naturally becomes a retrieval model, yielding two models from a single training run. \DIG is organized around a \emph{feature assignment taxonomy}: item-intrinsic static features are encoded into SIDs, user-item cross features (u2i) implicitly drive codebook boundaries toward recommendation decision boundaries during training, and an MLP$_\mathrm{u2t}$ distillation module approximates u2i at the token level for inference. Experiments on three public benchmarks and two industrial datasets demonstrate that \DIG simultaneously improves ranking, retrieval, and unified retrieval-ranking quality.

### [3] [Towards Self-Evolving Agentic Literature Retrieval](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14306)
*Yuwen Du,Tian Jin,Jing Kang,Xianghe Pang,Jingyi Chai,Tingjia Miao,Fenyi Liu,WenHao Wang,Sikai Yao,Yuzhi Zhang,Siheng Chen*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种自演化智能体文献检索系统PaSaMaster，解决了传统关键词检索无法捕捉复杂研究意图和大语言模型检索存在幻觉和高成本的问题，在多学科基准测试中取得了远优于现有方法的性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 大语言模型重塑科研的背景下，文献检索同时面临着保证来源真实性和深度理解学术搜索意图的双重挑战；传统基于关键词的检索无法捕捉复杂研究意图，前沿大语言模型虽然能处理复杂意图，但存在成本高和容易产生幻觉的问题，因此需要新的文献检索系统。

Method: 提出PaSaMaster自演化智能体文献检索系统，核心设计包括三点：1）将文献检索从一次性查询-文档匹配转化为随时间演化的搜索过程，利用已排序证据发现缺口、细化意图并指导后续搜索；2）将检索建模为意图-论文相关性排序而非生成任务，避免幻觉来源；3）通过规划与检索分离提升成本效率，仅用前沿大语言模型做意图理解，大规模检索和相关性打分由定制语料库和轻量级模型完成。在涵盖38个学科的PaSaMaster基准上进行评估。

Result: 实验发现传统关键词检索存在严重的不准确性和不完备性，PaSaMaster相比其F1分数提升15.6倍；生成式大语言模型幻觉率高达37.79%；PaSaMaster在保证零来源幻觉的前提下，性能比GPT-5.2高出30.0%，且仅需要1%的计算成本。

Conclusion: PaSaMaster解决了现有文献检索方法的核心缺陷，在多学科测试中实现了更准确、可靠且低成本的文献检索，实现了零来源幻觉，代码已开源。

Abstract: As large language models reshape scientific research, literature retrieval faces a twofold challenge: ensuring source authenticity while maintaining a deep comprehension of academic search intents. While reliable, traditional keyword-centric search fails to capture complex research intents. Frontier LLMs can handle complex research intents, but their high cost and tendency to hallucinate remain key limitations. Here we introduce PaSaMaster, a self-evolving agentic literature retrieval system that produces relevance-scored paper rankings with evidence-grounded recommendations through iterative intent analysis, retrieval, and ranking. It is built on three key designs. First, it transforms literature retrieval from a one shot query--document matching problem into a search process that evolves over time, using ranked evidence to reveal gaps, refine intents, and guide follow-up searches. Second, it prevents hallucinated sources by treating retrieval as intent--paper relevance ranking rather than generation. Finally, PaSaMaster improves cost efficiency by separating planning from retrieval: a frontier LLM is used only for intent understanding, while large scale retrieval and relevance scoring are delegated to customized corpora and lightweight models. Evaluated on the PaSaMaster Benchmark across 38 scientific disciplines, our system exposes the severe inaccuracy and incompleteness of traditional keyword retrieval (improving F1-score by 15.6X) and the unreliability of generative LLMs (which exhibit hallucination rates up to 37.79%). Remarkably, PaSaMaster outperforms GPT-5.2 by 30.0% at a mere 1% of the computational cost while ensuring zero source hallucination: https://github.com/sjtu-sai-agents/PaSaMaster

*Laleh Nourian,Anisa Callis,Stephanie Patterson,Jadeline Miao,Jamison Heard,Garreth W. Tigwell*

Main category: cs.AI

TL;DR: This paper explores how international students in the U.S. use conversational AI tools to assist with their cross-cultural adaptation, identifying current usage patterns, motivations, limitations, and providing recommendations for designing AI support tailored to their needs.


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Cross-cultural adaptation is highly stressful for U.S.-based international students, who face unique overlapping challenges, and the existing student support ecosystem is fragmented. While many students use conversational AI, there is a lack of clear understanding of how they adopt and perceive these technologies as adaptation support tools.

Method: A mixed-methods study was conducted: a survey of 60 international students was first used to map the relationship between students' adaptation challenges and conversational AI adoption patterns, followed by semi-structured interviews with 14 participants to explore underlying motivations and boundaries of AI use.

Conclusion: Based on the identified usage patterns, needs, and limitations of current conversational AI for supporting international students' cross-cultural adaptation, the paper provides actionable recommendations for developing customized AI-powered support tools that meet the unique needs of this population.

Abstract: Moving to a new culture and adapting to a new life, as an international student, can be a stressful experience. Our findings show that AI is perceived as a first-aid tool for immediate challenges, however, there is an interest in transforming AI from a tool for short-term help into a long-term support companion. By identifying where and how AI can provide long-term support, and where it is insufficient, we contribute recommendations for creating AI-powered support tailored to the unique needs of international students.

*Yuyan Chen*

Main category: cs.AI

  <summary>Details</summary>

Experiments against GPT-5.4, Claude Sonnet 4.6, Gemini 3.1 Pro, and other baselines demonstrate gains of 23% on informativeness and 18% on factual correctness, with consistent advantages in tactfulness and viewer engagement.

### [6] [RxEval: A Prescription-Level Benchmark for Evaluating LLM Medication Recommendation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14543)
*Shuhao Chen,Weisen Jiang,Changmiao Wang,Xiaoqing Wu,Xuanren Shi,Yu Zhang,James T. Kwok*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文提出了面向大语言模型（LLM）药物推荐任务的处方级基准RxEval，用于评估LLM选择匹配患者病程的具体药物-剂量-给药路径组合的能力，对16个LLM的评估结果显示该基准具有挑战性和区分度，现有前沿模型仍存在明显错误。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有住院患者药物推荐的基准都将任务建模为入院级别的粗粒度药物编码预测，无法反映实际临床中随患者病情变化逐时间点开处方的信息丰富性和真实任务特性，因此需要一个更贴合实际的评估基准。

Method: 将LLM药物推荐能力评估构建为多项选择任务，每个问题包含详细患者档案和按时间排序的临床轨迹，要求从真实处方中选择具体的药物-剂量-路径三元组，干扰项是通过推理链扰动生成的患者特异性干扰项；构建得到包含584位患者、1547个问题、覆盖18个诊断类别和969种独特药物的基准数据集RxEval，并在此基准上评估16个不同LLM的表现，再进行错误分析。

Conclusion: RxEval是一个贴合真实临床处方场景的LLM药物推荐评估基准，能够有效区分不同模型的能力，当前最优大语言模型在该任务上仍存在较大提升空间。

Existing benchmarks formulate this task as admission-level prediction over coarse drug codes with multi-hot diagnostic and procedure code inputs, failing to capture the per-timepoint, information-rich nature of real prescribing. We propose RxEval, a prescription-level benchmark that evaluates LLM prescribing capability by multiple-choice questions: each question presents a detailed patient profile and time-ordered clinical trajectory, requiring selection of specific medication-dose-route triples from real prescriptions and patient-specific distractors generated via reasoning-chain perturbation. RxEval comprises 1,547 questions spanning 584 patients, 18 diagnostic categories, and 969 unique medications. Evaluation of 16 LLMs shows that RxEval is both challenging and discriminative: F1 ranges from 45.18 to 77.10 across models, and the best Exact Match is only 46.10%. Error analysis reveals that even frontier models may overlook stated patient information and fail to derive clinical conclusions.
